UAE Sustainability Initiatives

How the Emirates leads the way in climate action

2050

UAE Net Zero by 2050

The UAE Strategic Initiative to achieve net-zero emissions by 2050, making it the first Middle Eastern nation to set this ambitious target. This comprehensive plan includes massive investment in renewable energy, green hydrogen, and carbon capture technologies.

Ongoing

Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum Solar Park

The world's largest single-site solar park, generating 5,000 MW of clean energy by 2030. Located in Dubai, this project will reduce 6.5 million tonnes of carbon emissions annually and power 1.3 million homes.

2030

Abu Dhabi's Masdar City

The world's most sustainable urban development, powered entirely by renewable energy. Masdar City showcases cutting-edge green building design, zero-waste principles, and sustainable transportation systems.

Active

Green Building Regulations

Mandatory green building codes across all new construction in Dubai and Abu Dhabi. Buildings must meet strict efficiency standards for water, energy, and materials, reducing environmental impact by up to 50%.
